Grilled Cheese Recipe

Recipe Description & Ingredient List

Recipe Description

Deliciously cheesy and crunchy grilled cheese sandwich recipe, perfect for all ages to enjoy.

Ingredients

  1. Cheese of your choice
  2. Mayo or butter
  3. Bread of your choice
  4. Optional: Tomato
  5. Optional: Bacon

Recipe Steps

  1. Take out two slices of the bread you have chosen.
  2. Apply butter/mayo on one side of each piece of bread.
  3. Place one side of bread butter/mayo side down in cold pan/skillet.
  4. Add cheese of your choosing to bread in the skillet and place other piece of bread on top butter/mayo side up.
  5. Turn stove top onto medium heat.
  6. Check with spatula if bottom side of sandwich is golden brown. If so flip the sandwich and proceed to grill other side until golden brown and cheese is melted.
  7. Enjoy your delciously cheesy grilled cheese!
