Chicken Parmesan

Recipe Description & Ingredient List

Recipe Description

Deliciously tender fried chicken topped with marinara sauce and mozzarella. With accompanied side of preferred pasta and marinara.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Tomatos
  • Box of Preferred Pasta
  • Mozzarella
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Two Eggs
  • Flour
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Paprika
  • Olive Oil

Recipe Steps:

  1. Fill a pot with water, add salt an olive oil and bring to a boil.
  2. As water is heating up in a small sauce pot add olive oil and saute onions and garlic until tender and fragrant.
  3. Once onion and garlic are soft, add crushed red tomatos from a can and freshly diced romano tomatos to the sauce pot.
  4. Bring sauce to a boil and lower heat and let simmer while frying your chicken.
  5. While water comes to a boil create flour, egg, and bread crumb bath.
  6. Butterfly chicken breast and roll with rolling pin until tender.
  7. Dip butterflied chicken breast in flour until fully coated, then proceed to dunk into egg bath, followed lastly by a dip in the bread crumb mixture.
  8. Once water has begun to boil add your pasta of choice into the pot until Al Dente.
  9. While pasta is boiling bring olive oil up to temperature and fry the covered chicken breast in the oil on both sides until crispy and golden brown.
  10. Once chicken has been fried on both sides pull from the pan and add a spoonful of tomato sauce on top of the chicken and then add a slice or two of mozzarella on top.
  11. Placed your completed chicken into the oven for 25-30 minutes at 425 fahrenheit until chicken has fully cooked and cheese has melted.
  12. Drain pasta in a collainder and drizzle olive oil on pasta and set aside for chicken to complete cooking.
  13. Place pasta on a plate and top with tomato sauce and parmesan cheese, then place fried chicken parm on top.
  14. Lastly, Enjoy your food!
